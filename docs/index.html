Powerful Features

Everything you need to enhance your Twitter experience, all in one extension.

User Remarks

Add personal notes to any Twitter user. Never forget why you followed someone or what they're known for.

Video Downloads

Download Twitter videos with a single click. Save your favorite content for offline viewing or sharing.

Batch Selection

When a tweet has multiple videos, select and download them all at once. No more clicking one by one.

Download Management

Keep track of all downloads with search, filter, and locate features. Find any video instantly.
